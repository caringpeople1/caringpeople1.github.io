Tip for at School:

Stop whatever you are doing to breath and calm yourself down

  • Sometimes you need to take a moment to recollect your thoughts and calm yourself down.
  • Also when you breathe deeply, it sends a message to your brain to calm down and relax.
  • You can close your eyes and try to visiualize yourself in a calm place. This can also help when it comes to preparing for presentation. Trying to imagine yourself presenting to prepare yourself.
