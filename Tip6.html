Tip for at School:

Take a walk outside of the Classroom

  • Find a place where you can be alone to recompose yourself.
  • You can take a trip to the bathroom to relax. Sometimes being away from the situation that is causing your stress can help clear your head.
  • If it's too overwhelming ask if you can go home for the day. Sometimes you need to take a break at home so you can come back to school calm and collected
