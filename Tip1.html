Tip #1

  • Take a deep Breath. Inhale for two seconds and exhale for 4 seconds.
  • Count to 10 while breathing and make sure your eyes are closed.
  • Let your thoughts go, at this moment don't think just feel.
  • Focus on one sound and listen to it.
  • Do not forget to breath.
  • If you are sitting choose a comfortable position. It is best to put your feet on the floor and your hands on your lap.
  • If you are lying down, relax your legs, arms and body. Face your palms to the ceiling.
