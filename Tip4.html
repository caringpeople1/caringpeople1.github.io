Tip #4

Breathe:

  • Slow, deep breaths can help lower blood pressure and heart rate.
  • Try breathing through one nostril at a time to relieve anxiety. The technique is supposed to work to balance your mind and body

Count Backward:

  • When worries are running rampant, try slowly counting to 10 and then back again to calm down.
  • It’s harder to freak out about an upcoming exam or job interview when you’re busy remembering what number comes before seven.

Be Alone:

  • Five minutes of alone time can help you collect your thoughts and clear your head.
  • Find a quite place like a small park or a coffee shop, where there are no people or not that much people so you can relax and be by youeself.

Treat Yourself:

  • Buy yourself some candies or a nice new dress, or shirt, or anything else that brings you joy.
  • However, do not spent too much money if it can ttrigger stress in the future.
